Introduction

Objective

In order to fully realize the potential of NCAA Division I intercollegiate wrestling for all involved parties, it is crucial to conduct periodic comprehensive assessments of the sport.

Although incremental changes are typically implemented to address specific concerns, the cumulative effect of these minor modifications over time can result in a lack of consistency and coherence within the sport, leading to its own set of issues.
