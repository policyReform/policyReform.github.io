Introduction

Objective

To ensure NCAA Division I intercollegiate wrestling is reaching its potential for all stakeholders, it is important to periodically perform a wholistic review of the sport. The regular process for reforms and changes are incremental to address specific issues but these minor changes over time can lead to a lack of uniformity and cohesion within the sport that causes its own issues over time.
